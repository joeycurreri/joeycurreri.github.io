About Joey Curreri

Joey Curreri is a 21-year-old jazz trumpeter based in Miami, FL and Los Angeles, CA.

He is currerntly finishing out his undergraduate degre at the University of Miami's Frost School of Music, studying with jazz trumpet luminary, Brian Lynch.

Joey writes for and leads his own group, the Joey Curreri Quintet, and they have released two EPs of original music on bandcamp.

Awards and Achievements

Since his late teens, Joey has been selected for and has won a number of awards and honors:

  1. Winner of 2nd place at the 2021 Carmine Caruso International Jazz Trumpet Solo Competition
  2. National Youngarts Foundation Jazz Trumpet Finalist and Winner (2018)
  3. Los Angeles Jazz Society's 2017 Shelly Manne New Talent Award Winner
  4. 2-time semifinalist in the National Trumpet Competition (2019 & 2020)
  5. Selected to the 2016 and 2017 Grammy Jazz Big Band, performing at events surrounding the Grammys, as well as jazz clubs in Los Angeles and New York
  6. Selected to the 2018 Next Generation Jazz Orchestra, which toured and performed in various schools and clubs in Japan
