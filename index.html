About

Joey Curreri is a 22-year-old jazz trumpeter based in Miami, FL, Los Angeles, CA, and New York, NY

He is a multi-award-winning trumpet player who has had the opportunity to perform alongside jazz greats such as Bobby Broom, Delfeayo Marsalis, Lisa Henry, Brian Lynch, John Beasley, Marcus Strickland, Etienne Charles, Jimmy Greene, Tia Fuller, Ingrid Jensen, Maria Schneider, and many more.

He has just finished his undergraduate degree at the University of Miami's Frost School of Music, studying with jazz trumpet luminary, Brian Lynch.

Joey will move to New York City in the fall to work and to complete a Masters degree at the Manhattan School of Music, studying under jazz trumpet legend Ingrid Jensen.

Joey writes for and leads his own group, the Joey Curreri Quintet, and they have released two EPs of original music, available for streaming and purchase on bandcamp.
