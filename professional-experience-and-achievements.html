Achievements and Awards

Awards

Since his late teens, Joey has been selected for and has won a number of awards and honors.

  • Winner of 2nd place at the 2021 Carmine Caruso International Jazz Trumpet Solo Competition
  • Downbeat Student Awards 2022 Outstanding Soloist, Undergraduate Combo Division
  • National Youngarts Foundation Jazz Trumpet Finalist and Winner (2018)
  • Los Angeles Jazz Society's 2017 Shelly Manne New Talent Award Winner
  • 2-time semifinalist in the National Trumpet Competition (2019 & 2020)
  • Selected to the 2016 and 2017 Grammy Jazz Big Band, performing at events surrounding the Grammys, as well as jazz clubs in Los Angeles and New York
  • Selected to the 2018 Next Generation Jazz Orchestra, which toured and performed in various schools and clubs in Japan
