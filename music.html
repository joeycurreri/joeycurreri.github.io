Joey Curreri Quintet

Joey has released two EPs of original music with his quintet formed during his time in Miami

EP

Simply titled, EP, this is Joey's first release with his band, an album that consists of five originals, available for purchase on Bandcamp.

EP was released on January 19, 2021

Click here to listen to EP

Personnel

  • Joey Curreri - trumpet
  • Dylan Mchann - tenor saxophone
  • Connor Rohrer - piano
  • Patrick Leavy - bass
  • Marcello Carelli - drums
  • Featured artist - David Mason - alto saxophone

Live at Clarke Recital Hall

As a final requirement for his degree in Studio Music and Jazz at the Frost School of Music, Joey presented a recital with his group on May 2, 2022

This recital debuted new music of Joey's, as well as showcased music by Mark Turner and Herbie Hancock.
